								Instructions!!!

                                                Press UP/DOWN Keys --> To Navigate The Sheet
                                                Press I --> To Input The Value in a Cell
                                                Press B --> To Select Starting Cell For Copy/Cut/Sum etc
                                                Press C --> To Select Ending Cell & Call Copy()
                                                Press X --> To Select Ending Cell & Call Cut()
                                                Press V --> To Paste The Copy/Cut Data
                                                Press Ctrl+A --> To Add Row Above Current Cell
                                                Press Ctrl+B --> To Add Row Below Current Cell
                                                Press Ctrl+R --> To Add Column Right of Current Cell
                                                Press Ctrl+L --> To Add Column Left of Current Cell
                                                Press Shift+R --> To Add a Cell & Shift Current Cell To The Right
                                                Press Shift+D --> To Add a Cell & Shift Current Cell To The Down
                                                Press Shift+L --> To Delete a Cell & Shift Current Cell To The Left
                                                Press Shift+U --> To Delete a Cell & Shift Current Cell To The Up
                                                Press Alt+R --> To Delete a Row
                                                Press Alt+C --> To Delete a Column
                                                Press Alt+R --> To Delete a Row
                                                Press Alt+C --> To Delete a Column
                                                Press Alt+S --> To Get The Sum of Your Desired Data
                                                Press Alt+A --> To Get The Average of Your Desired Data
                                                Press Alt+M --> To Get The Maximum of Your Desired Data
                                                Press Alt+N --> To Get The Minimum of Your Desired Data
                                                Press Alt+C --> To Get The Count of Your Desired Data
                                                Press Ctrl+S --> To Save The File
                                                Press Ctrl+W --> To Save The File & Exit
                                                Press Escape --> To Exit
